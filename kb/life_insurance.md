# ShieldBase Life Insurance

**Coverage Overview**
ShieldBase Life Insurance provides straightforward financial security for your loved ones. We exclusively offer **Term Life** insurance, avoiding the complexities of whole life policies.

**Coverage Levels (Terms)**
Instead of tiers, Life Insurance is structured by the length of the term:
- **10-Year Term:** Ideal for short-term financial commitments like a personal loan.
- **20-Year Term:** Great for parents wanting coverage until their children reach adulthood.
- **30-Year Term:** Excellent for covering long-term obligations like a mortgage.

*Coverage amounts can range from $100,000 to $1,000,000 without requiring an invasive medical exam.*

**What's Included:**
- A guaranteed lump-sum death benefit paid to your beneficiaries if you pass away during the active term.
- Terminal Illness Rider: Access a portion of your death benefit early if diagnosed with a terminal illness.

**What's Excluded:**
- Death by suicide within the first 2 years of the policy (the standard contestability period).
- Fraudulent applications or material misrepresentations regarding health history.
- Death resulting from illegal acts.

**Key Requirements:**
- Applicants must be between 18 and 65 years old.
- Coverage amounts must be requested in numeric values greater than zero.
- Health status directly impacts the premium rate. We classify health as "Excellent", "Good", or "Fair".
- Pre-existing conditions may limit coverage options and require specialized risk assessment.
