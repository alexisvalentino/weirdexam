# The ShieldBase Claims Process and Policies

Filing a claim with ShieldBase is designed to be radically simple and transparent. We trust you, which is why our automated system clears 90% of minor claims on the same day.

**Step 1: Document the Incident**
Take photos and write down any relevant information (like police report numbers or witness contacts) immediately after the incident. For home damage, take a video walkthrough before cleaning anything up.

**Step 2: File the Claim**
Log into your ShieldBase online portal or call 1-800-555-0199 to file your claim. 
**Filing Deadline:** You must file an auto or home claim within **30 days** of the incident. Life insurance claims can be filed at any time after the policyholder's passing.

**Step 3: Adjuster Review**
A dedicated claims adjuster will be assigned. For many auto and home claims, our AI adjuster can approve claims under $2,500 instantaneously. For larger claims, we may schedule an in-person or virtual inspection of the damage within 48 hours.

**Step 4: Resolution & Payment**
Once approved, claim payouts are disbursed within **3-5 business days** directly to your enrolled bank account or directly to the verified repair shop.

**Deductibles and Policies**
- **Deductible Payment:** You are responsible for paying your deductible (e.g., $500 or $1,000) before ShieldBase covers the remaining approved costs. If repairs cost $3,000 and your deductible is $500, we disburse $2,500.
- **Glass / Windshield:** Auto policies have a $0 deductible for windshield repairs to keep you safe on the road.
