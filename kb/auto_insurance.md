# ShieldBase Auto Insurance

**Coverage Overview**
ShieldBase offers robust auto insurance policies tailored to everyday drivers. We protect you, your vehicle, and others on the road.

**Pricing Tiers & Coverage Levels**
We offer three flexible tiers to fit your budget and needs:
1. **Basic Tier (Third-Party):** Meets state minimum requirements. Includes Bodily Injury Liability and Property Damage Liability. This is our essential third-party coverage plan.
2. **Standard Tier:** Includes Basic coverage plus Collision Coverage (pays for damage to your car after an accident) and Uninsured Motorist protection.
3. **Comprehensive Tier:** Our most popular plan. Includes Standard coverage plus Comprehensive Coverage (protects against theft, weather damage, falling objects, and vandalism) and roadside assistance.

**What's Included:**
- **Bodily Injury Liability:** Covers expenses if you cause an accident injuring someone else.
- **Property Damage Liability:** Covers damage you cause to another person's property.
- **Collision & Comprehensive:** Included in higher tiers for ultimate protection.

**What's Excluded:**
- General wear and tear or mechanical breakdowns.
- Intentional damage or illegal activities.
- Using your personal vehicle for ride-sharing (requires a separate commercial policy).

**Key Requirements:**
- Drivers must be at least 16 years old.
- The vehicle year must not exceed the current calendar year.
- Valid driver's license is required.
