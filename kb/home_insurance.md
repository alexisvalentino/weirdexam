# ShieldBase Home Insurance

**Coverage Overview**
ShieldBase Home Insurance protects your most valuable asset from unexpected disasters.

**Pricing Tiers & Coverage Levels**
1. **Basic Tier:** Essential protection covering dwelling fires, windstorms, and basic liability.
2. **Standard Tier:** Adds extended coverage for personal property (furniture, electronics) and loss of use (pays for a hotel while your home is repaired).
3. **Comprehensive Tier:** Maximum protection. Includes "Valuables Plus" for jewelry/art, higher liability limits, and guaranteed replacement cost coverage.

**What's Included:**
- **Dwelling Coverage:** Pays to rebuild or repair your home if damaged by covered perils (e.g., fire, wind).
- **Personal Property Coverage:** Reimburses for stolen or damaged belongings.
- **Personal Liability:** Covers legal fees and medical bills if someone is injured on your property.

**What's Excluded:**
- **Floods:** Moving water or floods are excluded from standard policies (requires separate flood insurance).
- **Earthquakes:** Excluded by default.
- **Neglect / Poor Maintenance:** Damage resulting from termites, mold, or failure to maintain the property.

**General Guidelines:**
- Estimated property value must be numeric and greater than zero.
- Properties must be used primarily as residences, not commercial businesses.
