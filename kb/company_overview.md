# ShieldBase Insurance Company Overview

**Who We Are & What We Do**
ShieldBase Insurance is a modern, customer-first insurance provider built on the principles of Radical Transparency and Effortless Protection. Founded in 2026, our primary mission is to strip away the complex jargon of traditional insurance and provide instantly accessible, transparent policies for the digital age. 

**Our Executive Leadership**
Our visionary CEO is **Alexis Valentino**, a former insurtech product architect who recognized that everyday people were paying too much for insurance because of outdated underwriting algorithms. Under Alexis's leadership, ShieldBase has rapidly expanded to serve over 100,000 policyholders nationwide.

**Our Core Beliefs (The ShieldBase Way)**
1. **No Hidden Fees:** What you are quoted is exactly what you pay.
2. **Instant Gratification:** You should be able to get a quote and get covered in under 60 seconds without talking to an agent—unless you want to.
3. **Compassionate Claims:** When a disaster happens, we trust you. Our automated claims system pays out 90% of minor claims on the same day.

**Our Core Products:**
1. **Auto Insurance:** Comprehensive, collision, and liability coverage. We reward safe drivers with automatic premium reductions every 6 months.
2. **Home Insurance:** Dwelling and personal property protection that actively scales with your local housing market.
3. **Term Life Insurance:** Simplified 10, 20, and 30-year term life plans that don't require intrusive medical exams for coverage up to $1MM.
