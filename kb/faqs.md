# ShieldBase Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Can I bundle my auto and home insurance?**
Yes! Bundling your auto and home insurance with ShieldBase can save you up to 15% on your total combined premiums. Our automated system calculates this discount instantly.

**2. Are there any discounts for safe drivers?**
Absolutely. We offer a 'Clean Record Discount' for drivers with no accidents or tickets in the past 5 years, dropping your auto premium by an additional 10%.

**3. Am I eligible for coverage?**
For Auto and Home: If you have a valid license and a registered address, you are eligible to apply.
For Life: Anyone between 18-65 residing in the United States is eligible to apply for term life, provided they pass our rapid risk assessment questionnaire.

**4. What if I miss a payment?**
We offer a 15-day grace period for missed payments before any automated cancellation procedures begin. We will text and email you reminders before your policy is impacted.

**5. How do I cancel my policy?**
You can cancel your policy at any time without a cancellation fee by calling our support line or submitting a request strictly online. Any unused premium will be refunded on a pro-rata basis directly to your payment method.

**6. Do you run a hard credit check?**
No. ShieldBase uses a soft credit inquiry to help determine your insurance score, which is completely transparent and does not negatively impact your credit rating.
